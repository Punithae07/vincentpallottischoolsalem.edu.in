Who Are We?

St. Vincent Pallotti Matriculation Higher Secondary School was founded in 1996 by The Pallottine Fathers and Brothers in India. The school is one of the few Catholic co - educational institutions in Salem. Situated on the Steel Plant Road, opposite to Govt. Mohan Kumaramangalam Medical College the school is just a 12 km. distance away from Salem town.

Managed by the The Pallottine Fathers and Brothers of the Our Lady of Good Health Province the school seeks to provide sound academics to its pupils, ensuring at the same time an opportunity to excel in sports, co-curricular and extracurricular activities. Sound Christian principles form the corner stone of the institution, while respecting all religions and inculcating strong secular principles amongst the students.
